Sorry I peed in the garage




Okay.


Maybe you remember this-
before I went in the garage,
you made me lunch.

peanut butter bread I didn’t like.
So I had a ton of orange juice.



My brother was taking a shower while I was watching
powerpuff girls and I really couldn’t wait..


I know I should’ve waited..










But wait.



LET ME EXPLAIN.


I thought it was going to be an okay idea because
in my mind I thought pee was like water..


You spill… it dries...it goes away.

I’m sorry I peed in the garage.
